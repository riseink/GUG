FAQ

When am I charged each month?

Accounts are charged at time of initial purchase. All recurring payments will be drafted the same DATE each month going forward. If you start on the 12th of a month, you will be charged on the 12th of every month.

When do I get my box?

All boxes ship the 1st week of each month.

What if I move?

If you have a change of address let us know by email and we will update your account.

What happens at the end of my subscription?

Your subscription will automatically renew for the same terms as your original subscription.

What if I change my mind after I sign up?

You have 48 hours to cancel after signing up. Email us and there's no obligation.

What if my box does not come?

You will receive a tracking number via email when your box ships. If you do not get your box, contact us and we will send another box to you immediately.

After I sign up, when do I get my first box?

We run on a monthly schedule. If you order by the last day of a month your box will be shipped around the 10th of the following month.

How do I terminate my subscription?

Email us 30 days prior to the end of your terms and we will cancel it.
